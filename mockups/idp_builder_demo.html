How AI Enhances IDP Building

Pain Point Addressed:

Creating meaningful Individual Development Plans (IDPs) is time-consuming for managers and often results in generic, ineffective plans that don't align with the 70-20-10 model for optimal learning.

AI Solution:

  • 70-20-10 IDP Builder: Analyzes an employee's profile (current role, skills, identified gaps, aspirations) and organizational data (available mentors, projects, courses).
  • Generates Balanced Draft: Automatically drafts a suggested IDP structured around the 70% (experience), 20% (exposure), and 10% (education) learning model.
  • Suggests Specific Activities: Recommends concrete actions for each category, linking to relevant internal resources like specific projects, potential mentors (using the matching engine), or LMS courses.

Example Scenario:

A manager clicks "Generate IDP" for Somchai (Sales Rep aspiring to Area Manager). The AI drafts a plan including: 70% - leading a small cross-functional sales initiative, 20% - shadowing the current Area Manager, 10% - completing the online "Strategic Sales Planning" course.

Benefits:

  • Saves Manager Time: Reduces IDP creation time significantly (Target: -80%).
  • Improves Plan Effectiveness: Creates more structured, actionable, and balanced development plans.
  • Aligns Development: Connects learning activities directly to skill gaps and career goals.
  • Utilizes Resources: Promotes relevant internal projects, mentors, and courses.
