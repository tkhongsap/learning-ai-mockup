Mentor & Gig Matching

Implementation files: mentor_match.html, mobility_recommender.html

Minimal data requirements:

  • Employee skills, goals, MBTI/DISC (opt.)
  • Mentor/project profiles (expertise, capacity, location)
  • Previous match feedback scores

How AI Enhances Matching

Pain Point Addressed:

Finding the right mentor or relevant short-term project (gig) is often manual, relies on personal networks, and can lead to inconsistent or suboptimal matches.

AI Solution:

  • Matching Engine: Analyzes multiple data points:
    • Mentees/Gig-Seekers: Enriched profile data (skills, identified gaps, career goals, learning preferences).
    • Mentors: Profile data (expertise, skills they can teach, mentoring style, availability, location).
    • Gigs: Project descriptions (required skills, duration, business unit, potential learning outcomes).
  • Calculates Match Score: Uses algorithms (e.g., cosine similarity for skills, collaborative filtering based on past successful matches) to calculate a relevance score for each potential mentor or gig.
  • Presents Ranked List: Shows the user a ranked list of the top matches, highlighting the reasons for the match (e.g., "Strong match on Strategic Planning skill", "Aligned career path experience").

Example Scenario:

Somchai clicks "Find Mentor". The AI analyzes his goal (Area Sales Manager) and skill gaps (Strategic Planning, Leadership). It identifies Supaporn (Key Account Mgr, strong in strategy, good feedback score) as a 92% match and Pichai (Regional Lead, direct experience but limited availability) as an 85% match.

Benefits:

  • Targeted Development: Connects employees with the precise resources needed for skill growth.
  • Wider Access: Exposes employees to mentors and projects beyond their immediate network.
  • Efficiency: Reduces time spent searching for opportunities or mentors.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Improves the quality and relevance of matches.
