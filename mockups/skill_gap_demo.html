How AI Enhances Dashboards

Pain Point Addressed:

HR and managers lack a clear, forward-looking view of talent readiness and potential attrition. Identifying skill gaps across teams is often manual and time-consuming. Executives need quick visibility into overall workforce skills.

AI Solution:

  • Skill-Gap Analyzer: (Used in IDP planning but visualized here) Compares current skills (from enriched profiles) against target role requirements to automatically identify and highlight gaps.
  • Skills Heat-Map: Aggregates skill data across business units or departments, visualizing strengths and weaknesses (Red/Yellow/Green) for quick executive overview.
  • Readiness & Flight-Risk Predictor (ML): Analyzes a combination of data (performance reviews, skill levels, engagement surveys, tenure, market trends) to score an employee's readiness for a future role (e.g., "Ready Now", "Ready in 6-12m") and predict their likelihood of leaving the company.
  • Auto-CDP Updater: (Background process) Consolidates data from various sources (LMS, PMS, 360 feedback) into a single, unified Career Development Profile timeline for each employee, ensuring data for predictions is current.

Example Scenario:

A manager reviews the dashboard for their team. The heatmap instantly shows a critical gap in "Strategic Planning". The readiness table identifies two "Ready Now" successors for a key role, but flags one as a "High" flight risk, prompting immediate retention action.

Benefits:

  • Proactive Succession Planning: Identify and develop future leaders based on data-driven readiness scores.
  • Targeted Retention Efforts: Focus on high-potential employees identified as flight risks.
  • Data-Driven L&D: Allocate training budgets effectively by focusing on the most critical, widespread skill gaps.
  • Strategic Workforce Insights: Provide executives with clear visibility into the organization's overall skill health and talent pipeline.
  • Real-time Data: Eliminates manual data consolidation, providing current insights.
